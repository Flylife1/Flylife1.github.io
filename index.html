It's been two years since I decided to go back to school. There have been a lot of obstacles but I am definitely happy with my decision. You see, a couple of years ago I was bartending when a friend of mine popped in, who I hadn't seen in years. We talked about what she had been up to and it turned out she needed a graphic designer for a project she was working on. Naturally, I offered my services and after she saw the work and approved, she paid me! But, that wasn't all. She needed a website. Like an idiot, I told her I could do it. I didn't have any experience whatsoever but I was always intrigued. So, I followed some tutorials and made my first website. It was horrible, but it was mine. Like an ugly first born child that only you see the beauty in. Anyway, I decided to go back to school to finish my graphic design degree and delve more into the world of web programming
